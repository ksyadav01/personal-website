KARAN.GG

KARAN.GG provides insight for League of Legends players and enhances their experience through analytics and statistics, providing players the information to improve and win more

League of Legends is a 5v5 MOBA developed by Riot Games in 2009 and played by millions daily around the world. Thanks to the massive playerbase and Riot's detailed API, websites by developers cropped up across the globe, pulling data and providing the hungry playerbase much-needed analysis. KARAN.GG is one of these, however it has key differences that seperates it from the competition.

Although KARAN.GG began as a simple side project, it quickly became much more than just a simple stat tracker and data puller. Using RIOT's API, KARAN.GG pulls standard information about players, matches, champions, etc. from their League of Legends games. However, it differs in what information it presents to the player. Unlike other stat-tracking websites, KARAN.GG provides statistics and information from recent games instead of a players entire history, giving the player more relevant information. KARAN.GG also allows the user the specify specific gamemodes and check their winrates with duo partners, something no other tracker has. With numerous other unique features, KARAN.GG is a powerful tool that helps players win more games. To test the power of this service, try some of the sample data or enter your own data.

Specific Champion Data


Provides comprehensive data for specific champions you have played in various queues in your past 100 games. It also provides your recent match history playing those respective champions.

Your Cumulative Data


Retrives your overall data, comprising of rank, lp, and wr for various queues.

Duo Checker


Finds whether 2 of the provided player igns have played games with eachother in various queues in your past 100 games, and if so provides your recent winrates.

Team Checker


Enter your champ select join data ("player1 has joined party, player2 has joined party, etc.") and this function provides comprehensive data of your team, including recent winrates, recent champions played in ranked, winrates with those champions, and more. Don't worry about entering names with any extra chat from the client, this function automatically ignores it!
